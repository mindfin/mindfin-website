The procedure of getting an Education Loan

Step 1: Fill out the Application Form.
As with any loan, the applicant must complete an application form that may ask for information such as contact information, academic information, and so on. It is critical not to provide incorrect information.
Step 2: Have a Personal Conversation.
The loan experts commit to a brief round of personal discussions to better understand your needs and to clarify the basic terms and conditions to you.
Step 3: Document Verification.
Before moving your application to the next stage, the bank or financial institution will thoroughly review all of the documents and documentation you have given.
Step 4: Credit Assessment.
The bank will put your application through a thorough credit process, which will involve the verification of your guarantor's creditworthiness, which is required in the case of an education loan.
Step 5: Signing the Contract.
The student is the actual borrower of the loan, while the parents/guardians are guarantors. The student must sign a promissory note to the bank once the loan has been approved.
Step 6: Disbursement of the Loan.
The bank will issue the loan once the formalities are fulfilled (additional paperwork and signed post-dated checks may be necessary). The bank may pay the college/institute fee directly to the institution in question.
