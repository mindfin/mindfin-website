Professional Loans


A professional loan is one that can be taken out by practitioners such as accountants, doctors, journalists, engineers, dentists, corporate secretaries, surveyors, attorneys, planners, business consultants, and consultants, as the term implies. Professionals who qualify for a professional loan must be eligible and have the necessary skillset to practice their specialty. It's comparable to a personal loan in that it's typically a no-collateral loan. It's worth noting that certain lenders might have a collateral condition, so consult with them first.


Professional Loans' Features


Professional loans have different characteristics depending on the bank. The following are some of the most recent features found in private banks: .

The professional Loans range in size from a few lakhs to as much as Rs 2 crores.

There are no necessary protection or collateral requirements. Any banks, however, can need it.

The loan has a maximum term of 5 years.

The technical loan can be used for a variety of things, including operating capital, equipment purchases, and office renovations.

The debt repayment is incredibly versatile. You have the option of repaying the loan through EMIs, post-dated checks, or an ECS Mandate.

The interest rate and loan amount are determined by the existence of the occupation or company. Doctors, Chartered Accountants, CS, CWA, and other professionals are examples.

Many banks provide different terms and conditions for businesses with audited financials and businesses without structured books of accounts. Professional loans can also be used to fund business-related travel costs such as airfare, lodging, conference fees, and so on.

The loan is usually given to the company owner in one lump sum. In certain circumstances, though, payment may be made directly to the vendors.


Professional loans are tailored to each individual's requirements, so there is a different loan for each professional:-


Loan for Doctors:

Doctor loans are available for up to $2 million. The loan sum will be disbursed in the borrower's account within 24 hours if all of the documents are correct. Banks also have simple EMIs and a variety of repayment options.


Loans for CA:

These loans are available to Chartered Accountants who need to fulfill their working capital needs. The amount of a CA loan varies by bank and is determined by a variety of factors such as the size of the company, the loan's intent, and the borrower's profile.


Eligibility criteria of Professional Loans


● Individuals between the ages of 18 and 65.
● People who are already licensed to practice their careers under state or federal legislation.
● A copy of their professional certificate indicating that they are licensed to practice the trade.
● chartered accountants,consultants, and other professionals with a certificate or degree certifying their competence.
● This form of loan is available to individuals, partnership companies, businesses, and sole proprietorships.


Documents Needed for a Professional Loan


● PAN Card – For a company, a firm, or a person(Aadhaar Card/Passport/Voters ID Card/Driving License/Pan Card are examples of KYC documents.)
● The previous six months Statement of Banks
● The most recent ITR, as well as revenue computations, balance sheets, and profit and loss accounts for the previous two years. A CA has attested to it.
● Proof of market continuity (ITR / Trade license / Establishment)
