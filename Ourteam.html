Our Team

Mindfin provides an environment for growth and immense opportunity. With over 100+ employees and several branches, Mindfin is the forerunner in dealing with Financial needs through Enthusiasm and Clarity. We are a dynamic and diverse team with deep product expertise and consumer expectations. At Mindfin, we are a team of keen listeners who discover straightforward solutions with patience and dedication.

Mr. Venkatesh. R

MD and Founder

Mr. Venkatesh. R, the company's MD and Founder, has developed and adopted a strategic approach to meet the industry standard. He has more than 20 years of experience and extensive insight and influence in this area. He has strong connections to senior administrators and business heads.

The idea of putting all banks, financial institutions, and resources under one roof was deduced from his large network with Banks, NBFC (non-banking financial services) and FinTech companies.

Mr. Venkatesh. R has a broad spectrum of connectivities with V.Ps, A.VPs, Zonal Sales Heads, National Sales Head, Zonal Credit Manager, National Credit Managers and FinTech MDs.

His association with various boards and establishments such as KASSIA, Bommasandra Industrial Association, Peenya Industrial Association, and tie-ups with building contractors, developers, and professionals has facilitated his years of experience, industry insights, and a strong understanding of the market.

He is the company's backbone and is instrumental in instilling its fundamental ideals and organisational standards.

Mr. Deepak A R

Director

An Alumni of Bangalore University, Mr. Deepak A.R is the Director, Sales and Marketing Head of Mindfin. His vast FMCG, Financial networks, and over a decade of Business expertise and experience are fundamental to the company’s Infrastructure and Management.

Mr. Deepak has inculcated the initiation, implementation, and execution of the Core Values and Vision of Mindfin.

Mr. Manuprasad H V

Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Manuprasad is the CEO of the company who offers mentorship and guidance with industry experience in Strategy Planning, Sales, Marketing, and Promotions.

He is the company's first point of contact. His functions and duties encompass strategic, financial, and organisational leadership, management tactics, and conflict resolution.
